Onboarding

After a thorough organizational scan, we introduce the Thrive vitality platform within your organization. We actively help to maximize the added value for the organization and all employees.

Employee insight

Employees gain immediate insight into their vitality through our self-scan and periodic check-ins.

3

Personalised offer

Employees work on their vitality through inspiring and personalized content such as webinars, master classes, practical guides, blogs and valuable products. Everything is focused on themes and topics that match the needs of the employee.

Access to our wellbeing experts

Employees are in direct contact with our wide range of wellbeing experts with a very wide variety of specializations and with both online and offline support.
